# Individuals and Motivation

## Basic idea

Motivation = the driving force pushing individuals to act. The two canonical organisational theories are Maslow's hierarchy of needs (motivation flows up the pyramid) and Herzberg's two-factor model (satisfaction and dissatisfaction have different drivers).

## Key facts

- Maslow: 5 levels — physiological → safety → belonging → esteem → self-actualisation.
- Herzberg: hygiene factors (pay, conditions, supervision) prevent dissatisfaction; motivators (achievement, recognition, growth) cause satisfaction.
- They are separate axes — fixing hygiene does not produce satisfaction.

* Motivation is derived from the word 'motive'
* Driving force which propels individuals to action.
