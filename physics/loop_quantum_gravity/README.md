# loop\_quantum\_gravity

## Basic idea

Background-independent quantisation of general relativity. Spacetime geometry is described by spin networks; area and volume operators have discrete eigenvalues. See also [[quantum_loop_gravity]] — same topic under an alternate name.

## Key formulas

- Wheeler-DeWitt equation: $\hat H \Psi = 0$
- Holonomy: $h_e[A] = \mathcal{P}\exp\!\left(\int_e A\right)$
- Area eigenvalues: $A = 8\pi\gamma\ell_P^2 \sum_i \sqrt{j_i(j_i+1)}$
- Volume eigenvalues: discrete spectrum at vertices of the spin network.
