# Loop Quantum Gravity

## Basic idea

Quantises spacetime geometry directly without assuming a background metric. Quantum states are spin networks; area and volume operators have discrete spectra.

## Key formulas

- Wheeler-DeWitt equation: $\hat H \Psi = 0$
- Holonomy: $h_e[A] = \mathcal{P}\exp\!\left(\int_e A\right)$
- Area eigenvalues: $A = 8\pi\gamma\ell_P^2 \sum_i \sqrt{j_i(j_i+1)}$
- Volume eigenvalues: discrete spectrum at vertices of spin network
