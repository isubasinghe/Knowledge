# Simone De Beauvoir

## Basic idea

French existentialist and feminist philosopher (1908-1986). Author of *The Second Sex*; extended Sartrean existentialism into an *ethics of ambiguity* and a foundational critique of gender as a social construct.

## Key tenets

- "One is not born, but rather becomes, a woman" — gender is constructed.
- Ethics of ambiguity: freedom is meaningful only when willed in solidarity with others' freedom.
- Woman has been historically defined as the Other against Man as Subject.
- Authentic existence requires resisting roles imposed by patriarchy and habit.
